How to Play

• You are Player 1 (red, O) — play against the Bot (blue, X).

• Click any empty circle to place your piece.

• After every move, the board rotates automatically.

• The outer ring and the inner square may rotate in different directions. The arrows will appear after the first move to reveal the rotation pattern.

• The goal is to create a line of 4 of your pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

• Because the board rotates, lines can form after the rotation, not just when the piece is placed.

• If both players form a line at the same time, the game ends in a draw.

• If all pieces are used and nobody forms a line, the game is also a draw.
